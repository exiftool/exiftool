ExifTool Meta Information Repository

The following archives contain sample JPEG images with the original meta information from 7092 different models of digital cameras, DV recorders, scanners and cell phones from 109 manufacturers. To conserve space and avoid copyright problems, the main image has been replaced, and the large preview image (and any other information found after the JPEG EOI) has been removed. However, the JPEG APP1 EXIF segment as well as any other APP segments are preserved in their original condition.

Note: Although an effort has been made to provide samples with original meta information, it is inevitable that some samples have been modified by other software. Whenever possible, such images are replaced in the repository as more suitable samples become available.
